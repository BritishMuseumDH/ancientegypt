Second Intermediate Period

The Second Intermediate Period was a period from about 1650 B.C. to 1550 B.C. when a group of Asiatic settlers (the Hyksos) gained power in the Delta region, and eventually took over the government.

This period ended when Ahmose I overthrew the Hyksos kings and began the 18th Dynasty.

It is with the 18th Dynasty that the 'New Kingdom' period begins.
