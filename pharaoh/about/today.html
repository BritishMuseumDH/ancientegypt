The Cast Today

The plaster cast of the wall reliefs from the Beit el-Wali temple is on display in the 'Egypt and Africa' room (Room 65) of the British Museum in London.


On display at the British Museum, London

If you visit the museum, see if you can find any objects in the gallery that are similar to objects shown in the Beit el-Wali relief.
