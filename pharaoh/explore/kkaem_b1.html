Why was Khaemwaset an important person in ancient Egyptian history?


Statue of Khaemwaset
Khaemwaset was interested in preserving the history of Egypt, and took part in restoring several temples which were ancient when he was alive! He was also a priest.

We know a lot about Khaemwaset because he was also a magician and there were many stories written about him.
