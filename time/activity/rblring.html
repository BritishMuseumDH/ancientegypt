Ring of Sheshonk I
Around 950 B.C. 
 
 


 

This ring shows the cartouche of the pharaoh Sheshonk. Sheshonk ruled ancient Egypt during the 22nd dynasty.

This is a special kind of ring called a bezel ring. The stone on a bezel ring twists around on the band.
