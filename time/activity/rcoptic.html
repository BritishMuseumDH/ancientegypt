Coptic Tapestry
Around A.D. 300 
 
 


 

This tapestry was made in Egypt in the early 4th century A.D.. It is made of linen and wool. Tapestries like this were woven in the Coptic Period (A.D. 395 to A.D. 641) showing scenes from mythology and popular stories from the time.
