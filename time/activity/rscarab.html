Green heart scarab
Around 1600 B.C. 
 
 


 

In 1125 B.C., a group of ancient Egyptian men were caught robbing tombs. Their trial was recorded on a papyrus that exists today. During the trial, the robbers admitted that they had taken the heart scarab shown in this picture.
