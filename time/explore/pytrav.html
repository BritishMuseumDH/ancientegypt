Travellers and explorers

In 1765, an Englishman named Davison found first chamber above ‘King's Chamber' in the Great Pyramid.

In 1818, an Italian named Giovanni Belzoni found entrance to Khafre's pyramid and explored the inside.

In 1837, an Englishman named Howard Vyse found four more chambers above ‘King's Chamber' in the Great Pyramid.
