Cleopatra VII

Cleopatra VII (commonly known as just ‘Cleopatra') is one of the most famous queens of Egypt. Cleopatra was well-educated and spoke many languages including Egyptian. In fact, she was the only ruler during the Ptolemaic period who actually spoke the Egyptian language (Greek was the official language of government in Egypt during the Ptolemaic period).

Cleopatra is also well-known for her love affairs with Julius Caesar and Mark Antony.
