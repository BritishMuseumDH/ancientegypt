The Challenge - ‘Tools of the Trade' 
 
 

Craftsmen in ancient Egypt used many different kinds of tools to do their work.

  

Instructions:
Look at the tools and read the cards that describe them. Match the tools with the craftsmen by clicking on the empty boxes. If you change your mind, just click on the box again to return the card to the pile.

When you have put a card in each empty box, the computer will work out how many you got right. Keep trying until you get them all right.
